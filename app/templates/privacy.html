Privacy Policy – SkyAI

This Privacy Policy outlines how SkyAI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, protects, and shares personal information of users (“you”) who access or use the SkyAI platform.

1. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name and email address
  • Date, time and location of birth
  • User account information and preferences
  • Responses to astrological and numerological inputs
  • Technical data such as device type, IP address, browser, etc.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized astrological and numerological reports
  • Improve your experience and suggest relevant insights
  • Communicate with you (e.g., welcome messages, support)
  • Process payments securely (when applicable)
  • Ensure platform security and performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with third parties strictly necessary for:

  • Providing AI-generated insights (e.g., OpenAI)
  • Processing secure payments (e.g., Stripe)
  • Hosting and infrastructure services (e.g., Render, Firebase)
  • Legal or regulatory compliance, when required

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected using modern encryption and access control practices. Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. User Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us. You also have the right to withdraw consent and disable account usage.

6. Cookies

SkyAI uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can configure your browser settings to block cookies, but this may impact platform functionality.

7. International Users

By using SkyAI outside Brazil, you agree that your data may be processed and stored in jurisdictions that may not offer the same level of protection as your country.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through our website or email. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of changes.

9. Contact

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: skyai@skyai.digital
